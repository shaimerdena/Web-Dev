Pancakes

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
flour 1 ½ cups
baking powder 3 ½ teaspoons
sugar 1 tablespoon
salt ¼ teaspoon
milk 1 ¼ cups
melted butter 3 tablespoons
large egg 1

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
Step 2: Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
Step 3: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
Step 4: Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
Step 5: Serve and enjoy!

Cucumber Sandwich

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
dill 1 handful
cucumber ½
cream cheese 3 tablespoon
lemon zest ½
lemon juice 1 teaspoon
salt a pinch
black pepper a pinch

Step 1: Cut the cucumber into thin, round slices. For a twist, add some lemon and dill into the recipe. Simply mix ½ teaspoon of lemon zest and 1 teaspoon of lemon juice into the cream cheese mixture.
Step 2: Evenly spread about a tablespoon of cream cheese on each slice of bread. Layer the cucumber slices neatly on one slice of bread and season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper.
Step 3: Top with the remaining slice of bread to form a sandwich. Press gently to ensure it holds together. For a traditional style, cut the crusts of as well. (You can keep the crusts in the freezer and use for croutons at a later date.) Slice the sandwich in half and enjoy!
